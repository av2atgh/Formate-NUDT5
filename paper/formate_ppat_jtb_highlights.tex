\noindent\textbf{An allosteric metabolite glue couples AICAR inversely to
one-carbon availability: a theoretical prediction and its therapeutic
corollaries}

\medskip

\noindent Alexei Vazquez

\bigskip

\begin{itemize}[leftmargin=1.5em,itemsep=0.5em]
\item A glue-regulated PPAT step replaces demand-slaved purine synthesis in the
formate switch model.
\item The model predicts AICAR falls as one-carbon availability rises --- an
inverse coupling.
\item The inverse coupling requires the PPAT--NUDT5 glue; a NUDT5 knockout should
reverse its sign.
\item Calibration to binding data alone reproduces AICAR and PRPP data from two
independent labs.
\item Corollaries for AMPK signalling and thiopurine/antifolate cytotoxicity are
derived.
\end{itemize}
