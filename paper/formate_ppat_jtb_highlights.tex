\noindent\textbf{An allosteric metabolite glue couples AICAR inversely to
one-carbon availability: a theoretical prediction and its therapeutic
corollaries}

\medskip

\noindent Alexei Vazquez

\bigskip

\begin{itemize}[leftmargin=1.5em,itemsep=0.5em]
\item Glue-regulated PPAT replaces demand-slaved purine synthesis in a formate model
\item AICAR falls as one-carbon availability rises --- an inverse coupling
\item The inverse coupling needs the PPAT--NUDT5 glue; NUDT5 loss reverses its sign
\item Calibration to binding data alone reproduces AICAR and PRPP data
\item Corollaries follow for AMPK signalling and thiopurine cytotoxicity
\end{itemize}
